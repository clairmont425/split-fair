## Brand Voice

**Tone direction:** Editorial/Utilitarian with warmth

The core tension: rent splitting is awkward and emotional, but math is objective. Split Fair makes an uncomfortable conversation into an undeniable fact. Tone is confident, slightly dry, smart — like a friend who happened to get a math degree.

- "Fair rent for every room" *(current — clean, clear)*
